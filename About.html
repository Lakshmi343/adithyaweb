Why Choose Us

Welcome to Adithya’s family – Quality Builders in Thodupuzha

ADITHYA BUILDERS involved in construction of all types of buildings and total project consulting for large scale projects.Our long-standing reputation is built on our quality approach, our passion for innovation and our professional, trusted relationships with clients.

We made our humble introduction in the year of 2000 at Thodupuzha. We had to face many challenges after getting introduced and we could overcome all of those by Gods grace and our team work. That experience made us capable to deliver quality work on time with100% client satisfaction. Our reputation is our happy and satisfied clients who are helping us grow further

ADITHYA BUILDERS has started by two civil engineers Er. Viju VV and Er. Abi PN. who were graduated in the year of 1995 from Mar. Athanasius college of engineering, Kothamangalam.Adithya builders is headed by Er. Abi PN who is the co- founder and Managing Director of the firm.

Whether it’s a refurbishment, conversion or new build of a residential or commercial property, we bring over 24 years experience to your door, backed up with the talent for traditional building techniques that makes ADITHYA the choice of both architects and clients across the region
